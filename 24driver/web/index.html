24 Drive

 

Dashboard for the 24 channel motor driver. PID control 24 DC-motor & potentiometer drive units, and sense with 12 strain gauges.

Drivers

Manually power the motors by holding down the power sliders (they reset to 0 when let go). Or seek position via PID control. The graphs show 5 seconds of data as seen by the drive chip. Position and seek are shown in the top graph, along with min and max limits dash lines. The bottom graph shows drive power (which linearly combines manual power and PID control), and motor current; with dashed lines representing the 0 axis.
